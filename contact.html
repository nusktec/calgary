    Our  Departments

    Children Church

    In Destiny House Children’s ministry, we are focused on bringing the word and ways of God to the children and teaching them to pray at their different levels. With the aim of raising Godly children in partnership with parents. Who will become great men and women of God tomorrow? Every child is loved and welcomed. God is doing amazing things.

    Youth Church

    The youth wing of the Church, officially referred to as ‘Dunamis Manchester Youth’, is composed of young adults between the ages of 15 and 24. Currently, the Youth Department meets for Sunday Church Services at least twice a month where messages delivered  are centred on their peculiar needs and specific issues.

    Women of Worth

    Dunamis Manchester Women of Worth:
     • Are committed to the teaching of the Word received at the altar of Dunamis International Gospel Centre.
     • Find their true identity, true worth and completeness in Christ alone. Together, we seek progressively greater revelation of who we are in Christ.

    Excellent Men

    Dunamis Calgary Excellent Men:
     • Are committed to the teaching of the Word received at the altar of Dunamis International Gospel Centre.
     

    Foundation Class

    Foundation/Membership class is tailored toward those that want to become members of the church or those that just gave their life to Christ. It also teaches what the church believes in.

    Maturity Class

     DISCIPLESHIP and LEADERSHIP TRAINING CLASS (DLTC) or Maturity class is the training arm of the ministry where people are disciplined to be grounded in God

    Dunamis (Doo’na-mis) is the Greek word that means POWER.  It is a power source that reproduces itself without reduction in intensity.
